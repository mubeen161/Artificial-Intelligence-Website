Machine Learning

Machine learning is a branch of artificial intelligence (AI) and computer science which focuses on the use of data and algorithms to imitate the way that humans learn, gradually improving its accuracy.Through the use of statistical methods, algorithms are trained to make classifications or predictions, and to uncover key insights in data mining projects.
